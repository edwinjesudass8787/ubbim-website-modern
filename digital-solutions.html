Our Process

How we build your bespoke solution

A proven, collaborative approach that keeps you involved at every stage — from discovery to deployment and beyond.

01

Discovery & Strategy

Deep-dive workshops to understand your workflows, pain points, data architecture, and stakeholder needs. We map the problem before we architect the solution.

02

Design & Prototyping

Wireframes, user journeys, and interactive prototypes. We validate the UX with your team before writing a single line of production code.

03

Build & Integrate

Agile development sprints with fortnightly demos. We integrate with your existing ERP, BIM, IoT sensors, and legacy databases.

04

Deploy & Optimise

Cloud or on-premise deployment, staff training, documentation, and a dedicated support retainer for continuous improvement.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does a typical bespoke project take?
It depends on scope, but most MVPs are delivered within 3–4 months. Complex enterprise platforms may take 6–12 months. We break projects into fortnightly sprints so you see progress continuously.
Do we own the source code?
Yes — full IP transfer is standard. You receive complete source code, documentation, and deployment credentials. No vendor lock-in. Ever.
Can you integrate with our existing systems?
Absolutely. Integration is core to what we do. Whether it's a 20-year-old legacy database or a modern cloud API, we architect robust connectors that keep your data flowing securely.
What happens after launch?
We offer flexible support retainers — from basic maintenance to continuous feature development. Most clients stay with us for years because the product evolves as their business does.
